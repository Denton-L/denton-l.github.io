About

Hello, my name is Denton Liu and I am a student studying Software Engineering at the University of Waterloo!

As a programmer, I enjoy understanding how things work. Most of the time, this means going underneath all of the layers of abstraction and just learning how systems work on the inside. When I'm not busy working on schoolwork or doing a programming project, I like to read about the internals of systems and how they are implemented. Sometimes, it just blows my mind how massive systems can be reduced into such simple parts.
